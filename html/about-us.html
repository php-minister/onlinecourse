About AlquranNow.com

AlQuranNow.com is a source of knowledge for those who are on the quest to learn the Quran and teach their children likewise. We have made ourselves available online in order to serve those people of the Muslim ummah who live in parts of the world where it isn’t as easy to get access to good Islamic teachers. AlQuranNow serves as a solution to your needs of learning the Qur’an

Who We Are

AlQuranNow is a platform developed by people interested in spreading the knowledge of the Quran. Supervised by Sheikh Saad Nomani, the teachers at AlQuranNow are by far of the highest quality standards and believe in the importance of learning the Quran in the most right way possible.

Our Vision & Mission

Al Quran Now strongly believes in the spread of Islamic knowledge in the most positive light. We envision a highly educated Islamic society in the future and in order to acquire that it is very important to spread the light of the Quran in every corner of the world where Muslims reside.

Teaching Standards

We provide Qur'an learning opportunity with correct accent and Tajweed. Our tutors are well-qualified Hafiz-e-Qur'an with complete know-how of Tajweed and Tarteel; have excellent command over Arabic, English, Punjabi and Urdu Languages for ease in communication with their students; are tested and approved by Sheikh Qari Saad Nomani himself. Apart from above mentioned

Our Quran Tutors are:

Well aware with the rules of Recitation.

Believe in student-centered teaching.

Trained from educational organizations.

Highly motivated and understand the complexities faced in this profession.

Management Team

alQuranNow.com is non-partisan organization, we are not part of any group, Islamic organization or mosques. Our focus is getting true teaching Qur'an across to whoever wishes to understand the true meaning of Qur'an and it's teachings. Our management team is based on well known personalities with great experience in there respective fields. We have qualified teachers, accountants , IT specialists and Technicians to ensure that our students face no difficulties in attaining answers during their learning.

We believe in student individuality and try to understand their learning styles in order to be able to provide conducive environment to their learning, motivating them to read Qur'an and related Islamic literature. All our tutors go through the screening of CRB (Criminal Record Bureau ) ensuring a safe environment for our learners.
