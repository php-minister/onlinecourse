About Us

AlQuranNow.com is a source of knowledge for those who are on the quest to learn the Quran and teach their children likewise. We have made ourselves available online in order to serve those people of the Muslim ummah who live in parts of the world where it isn’t as easy to get access to good Islamic teachers. AlQuranNow serves as a solution to your needs of learning the Qur’an.
